Dorelle Li

Timer Speaker

Fall 2025

Redesign, Human-Centered, Interactive, Digital experience, Edible?, To live easier

Challenge:
Nighttime listening for audiobooks, podcasts, or white noise often requires navigating apps and screens to set timers, adding unnecessary friction and screen exposure before sleep.

Design:
Designed a bedside timer–speaker that combines Bluetooth audio with a simple dial-based physical timer. The tactile interface removes the need for app-based controls, while a soft knitted wool covering gives the object a warm, calming presence suitable for a bedroom environment. The product was modeled and rendered in Rhino.

Outcome:
Creates a more restful nighttime routine by replacing screen interactions with an intuitive physical interface, turning a common digital task into a simple, comforting bedside experience.
