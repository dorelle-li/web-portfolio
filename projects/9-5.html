9-5

Fall 2025

Redesign, Human-Centered, Interactive, Fabrication and Craft, Play
  • Group members:
  • Anika Gupta, Junkyung Kim, Katherine Yoon

9–5 is a semi-collaborative board game exploring burnout and collective care within corporate life, drawing inspiration from The Game of Life to reimagine the workday as a shared journey rather than a competition. Developed in response to the community challenge of integrating systems of care to prevent burnout, the project uses humor, role-based play, and stress-tracking mechanics to model how stress moves through an office ecosystem. Through iterative design and playtesting, individual actions are translated into shared consequences, reframing success as collective well-being and encouraging empathy within systems that often wear people down.
