Challenge:
Address burnout in corporate environments by exploring how systems of care could be integrated into everyday work culture. The project responds to a community challenge focused on preventing burnout and rethinking individualistic workplace dynamics.

Design:
Designed 9–5, a semi-collaborative board game inspired by The Game of Life that reimagines the workday as a shared journey rather than a competition. Through humor, role-based play, and stress-tracking mechanics, the game models how stress moves through an office ecosystem. Iterative design and playtesting translated individual actions into collective consequences.

Outcome:
Reframes success as collective well-being rather than individual achievement. The game encourages player to communicate on collective experiences and through so be aware of how workplace behaviors affect others, prompting reflection on systems that often contribute to burnout.
