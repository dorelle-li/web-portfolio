Camera Bag

Fall 2025

Human-Centered, Fabrication and Craft, To live easier

This project reimagines the traditional camera bag by prioritizing efficiency, lightweight construction, and aesthetics. Designed for casual outdoor wandering, it functions as a decorative wearable that allows the camera to be quickly pulled out and returned during use. A two-part structure separates the camera from an outer storage compartment, giving users flexibility in how they carry their gear. The camera hooks with the inner bag to prevent accidental dropping while moving. The bag was developed over four weeks through iterative making, with all materials and hardware self-sourced.
