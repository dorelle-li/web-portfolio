Easy SPAM

Fall 2024

Redesign, Human-Centered, Fabrication and Craft, Edible?

This redesign of the classic SPAM container makes it easy to open and reseal while maintaining the original’s stackable, simple form. Inspired by push popsicles and Pringle cans, Easy SPAM features a lid, foil covering, and a movable plate that pushes the contents out without hitting the surface. The plunger stick can be secured at the back to reduce storage space. The project was modeled in SolidWorks and prototyped in 3D printing and foam.
