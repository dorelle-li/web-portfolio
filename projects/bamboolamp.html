Dorelle Li

Bamboo Lamp

Spring 2025

Material-driven, Fabrication and Craft

Challenge:
Explore how material research can drive design while finding alternative ways to reuse unwanted natural materials for sustainability. Understand the physical properties, strengths, and limitations of reclaimed bamboo.

Design:
Conducted iterative experiments with bamboo sourced from Seven Arrows Farm in Providence, testing various forms, structures, and combinations. Insights from these hands-on explorations informed the creation of a final lamp that directly reflects the material's characteristics.

Outcome:
Produced a functional lamp that embodies the results of material-driven research, demonstrating how experimental making can translate deep material understanding into a tangible, sustainable, design-forward object.

Bamboo was processed into standardized units to test flexibility, strength, and modularity—laying the foundation for iterative exploration.
