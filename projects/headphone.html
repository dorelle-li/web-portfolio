"Spagehetti" Headphone

Fall 2025

Redesign, Edible?

Inspired by the K-pop group LE SSERAFIM’s song Spaghetti, this headphone redesign captures the track’s dynamic energy and playful, retro vibe. Developed as a modeling and rendering exercise, the project translates the music’s whimsical character into form,color, material, and lighting.
