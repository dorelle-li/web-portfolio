Dorelle Li

"Spagehetti" Headphone

Fall 2025

Redesign, Edible?

Challenge:
Translate the playful, retro energy of the LE SSERAFIM song Spaghetti into a physical product design while practicing 3D modeling and rendering techniques.

Design:
Developed a conceptual headphone design that captures the song's whimsical character through expressive form, bold color, and stylized materials. The project focuses on translating musical mood into visual and physical elements, exploring how lighting, texture, and shape can convey the track's dynamic and playful atmosphere. The headphones were modeled and rendered as a design visualization exercise.
